About Oxorio

In the middle of cryptowinter, when classical fintech has nothing to lose but its chains, yet only dust is settled on the mainnet, our team is building viable industrial solutions on the interim of the two worlds.

We have solved the blockchain trilemma bottleneck (decentralisation, security, scalability) and applied zero-knowledge proofs to safeguard the sacred grail of traditional banking - user privacy in every transaction.
